About us

Our goal

We are FLL (Fast Language Learning). Our company specializes in the field of technology and language learning. Our company’s main goal is to help people learn language in a short period of time. We do so, because we believe that education and communication is an important part of every person's life to succeed, that is why we want to focus on developing technology that provides learners interactive education. FLL stands for Fast Language Learning. It is a company that specializes in Robotic learning tools with the field of expertise in interactive language learning. We therefore strive for designing better solutions while trying to solve problems such as the effectiveness, time-consumption, safety, and user-friendliness by providing interactive robots that will make language learning easier and fun. As a company we help learners to learn a language by talking. In fact, our motto is also “ learning by talking” FLL consists of four group members: Adrian Langbråten Stensrud, Zeray Tewelde Kahsay, Kennet Gjerstad Kløvstad, Mats Buraas Bergersen and Siri Esteri Berg-Johnsen all from group B3.
